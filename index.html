The Perfect Top Ramen

Description

  • Top Ramen is very easy to make and very tasty, especially when done correctly.

Ingredients

  • Half cup of water.
  • One packet of Top Ramen.
  • A pot.

Directions

  1. Pour water in the pot and put on the stove at medium heat.
  2. Open Top Ramen packet, take out the seasoning packet. Tear open the seasoning packet and pour it in the pot.
  3. When the water comes to a boil, pour the noodles into the water.
  4. Stir your noodles once in a while until almost all the water is gone.
  5. Pour noodles into a bowl and enjoy!
